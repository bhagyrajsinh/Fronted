Gujarati culture is known for its flavorful and balanced cuisine, and some famous traditional dishes include Dhokla,Undhiyu, and Thepla. Dhokla is a soft steamed snack made from fermented rice and gram flour batter, seasoned with mustard seeds and green chilies.

Undhiyu is a rich mixed-vegetable curry cooked with spices, fenugreek dumplings, and oil, especially popular during winter and festivals like Uttarayan.

Thepla is a thin flatbread prepared using wheat flour, fenugreek leaves, and spices, commonly eaten with yogurt or pickle while traveling or for breakfast.
